Our Story

Discover the passion and tradition behind our culinary creations

From Sapkota De Cafe

Founded in 2022, Sapkota De Cafe began as a small bakery with a single wood-fired oven. Our founder, Chef Umesh Sapkota, had a vision to create artisanal breads using traditional methods passed down through generations of ancestors.

Today, we've grown into a full-service restaurant and bakery, but our commitment to quality and tradition remains unchanged. We use only organic, locally-sourced ingredients and bake everything fresh daily in our signature wood-fired ovens.
